About Our Company

The Smart Waste Classification System is a technology-driven platform designed to improve waste management through intelligent classification and efficient disposal systems.

Our goal is to promote environmental sustainability by helping individuals and organizations properly sort and dispose of waste using AI-powered solutions.

We aim to reduce environmental pollution, encourage recycling, and contribute to cleaner, smarter cities through innovation and automation.

By combining machine learning with user-friendly interfaces, we provide a reliable system that simplifies waste sorting and supports a greener future.
